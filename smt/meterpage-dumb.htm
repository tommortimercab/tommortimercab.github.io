What this means

Your meter isn’t working in smart mode. This is because your supplier isn’t connected to the UK’s smart meter network yet.

You should take regular meter readings and send them to your supplier. If you don’t your supplier could send you estimated bills.

If you normally top up using an app you should ask your supplier how to do this.

If you want your meter to work in smart mode

You have a first generation (SMETS1) meter that’s been upgraded.

It’s worth asking your current supplier when they’ll support your meter working in smart mode.

You could also switch to a supplier that will support your meter working in smart mode.
