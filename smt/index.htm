Check your smart meter

Use this tool to find out what type of smart meter you have in your home and if it’s working in ‘smart mode’.

Smart mode means your meter can automatically send readings to your supplier, and if you have a prepayment meter you can top up using an app.

If you’re thinking of switching supplier, the tool can also tell you if your meter will work in smart mode after you switch.

Find this 12-digit number on your meter or energy bills. It’s also known as a meter point administration number or meter point reference number
