Data protection management checklist

Reviewed: 2 July 2019

If you’re responsible for the overall management of data protection processes in your local office, use this checklist to help you make sure your office is doing all it can to:

  • keep client, staff, volunteer and trustee data safe
  • meet membership requirements through the Leadership Self Assessment
  • comply with the GDPR and Data Protection Act

If you’re starting up a new Citizens Advice local office, you’ll need to register with the ICO.

You can use this checklist to help you carry out an annual review of your policies and procedures. You might want to do this to show GDPR compliance for your leadership self assessment or for reporting to an AGM or trustee board meeting.
