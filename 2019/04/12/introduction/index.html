* Introduction

If you’re a permanent or fixed-term employee, you get annual leave. The annual leave year starts on 1 January and ends on 31 December.

Each leave year, full-time employees get:

  • 26 days’ annual leave
  • 8 bank holidays
  • 4 Citizens Advice days when national offices shut – they’re usually between Christmas and New Year

After 3 years, you’ll get an extra day of annual leave each year – up to a maximum of 5 extra days. Your extra day will be added on 1 January.

If you work part time, your leave will be in hours. You can use the leave calculator to work out how much leave you’ll get.

If you joined Citizens Advice before 2004 you might get more leave – check your contract to find out how much you get.
