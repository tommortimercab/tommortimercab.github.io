Booking annual leave

You need to book annual leave on HRevolution.

If you have problems using HRevolution, you can email HRevolution@citizensadvice.org.uk.

Your line manager will get an email when you request leave. They’ll need to approve your leave before you can take the time off. Before approving your leave your line manager will check:

  • how long you want off – you should talk to them if you want to take 3 or more weeks at one time
  • if other people are going to be off at the same time and how this will affect the work the team can do
  • if any work deadlines will be affected by your holiday

It’s a good idea to let your line manager know that you want to take time off as soon as you can.
