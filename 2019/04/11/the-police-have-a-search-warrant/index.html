The police have a search warrant

You’ll have to contact the operations team if the police have a search warrant – don’t give out any information about a client.

Case notes and other confidential information about clients are usually classes as ‘legal professional privilege’. This means police won’t be able to see the information even if they have a search warrant – it’d come under something known as ‘excluded materials’.

They’d only be able to see the notes if a court decides they’re not covered by legal professional privilege but this is unlikely and it wouldn’t happen immediately.
