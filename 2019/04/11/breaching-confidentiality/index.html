* Introduction

There might be situations where you think you need to breach a client’s confidentiality.

You won’t be able to decide this yourself – you’ll need to follow certain rules depending on your sitaution.

For most situations you’ll need to contact the Citizens Advice operations team for help. They’ll need to authorise most breaches of confidentiality.
